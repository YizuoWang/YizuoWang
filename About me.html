Hailing from Shenzhen, I've embarked on a odyssey of self-discovery.


A pivotal chapter in my journey unfolded 2022 summer when I ventured into the bustling heart of New York City. It marked a profound milestone in my 23-year adventure. Amidst the vibrant chaos of this metropolis, I endeavored to comprehend the beauty of diversity and the significance of inclusion. I discovered that each person carries their own unique story, and I resolved to create my own narrative.


I began to embrace the art of living in the present moment—braving rainy days without an umbrella, becoming a regular patron of opera, ballet, and concerts with rush tickets, and randomly boarding the nearest train. These cherished moments make me feel alive in the world.


In the blink of an eye, I find myself capturing stories.


And now, I'm here.


"Cogito ergo sum" — René Descartes
